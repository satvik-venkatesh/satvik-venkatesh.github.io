About Me

Satvik holds a Bachelor of Technology in Information and Communication Technology from SASTRA, India, and a Research Masters in Computer Music from ICCMR, University of Plymouth. He currently is studying for a PhD in ICCMR on the topic of audio segmentation and intelligent mixing for live radio broadcast. His research interests include Brain-Computer Music Interfaces, Unconventional Computing, and Artificial Intelligence for music. Satvik is also an accomplished musician and performer.
